RICARBALL

A GAME MADE BY HYT

EXCLUSIVE ON PC

INSTRUCTIONS

PRESS THE PAUSE/RESUME-BUTTON TO PAUSE THE GAME

OR PRESS [ ENTER ] ANYTIME TO PAUSE AND RESUME THE GAME

AND YES, THE BALL IS RICARDO'S ASS

HOW TO PLAY

PRESS ↑ TO MOVE YOUR PADDLE UP

PRESS ↓ TO MOVE YOUR PADDLE DOWN

PRESS [ SPACE ] TO HIT THE BALL

SCORE 7 POINTS TO WIN
